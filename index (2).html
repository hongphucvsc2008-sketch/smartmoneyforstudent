Credit & Debt ‚Äì Borrow Smart

Credit is borrowed money you must repay, usually with interest.

  • Understand what a credit score is and why on-time payments matter.
  • Learn why paying only the minimum balance makes debt grow fast.
  • Example: See how $100 at 20% interest becomes $120 quickly.

Banking ‚Äì Keep Your Money Safe

Know the difference between chequing accounts and savings accounts.

  • Chequing: for everyday spending using a debit card.
  • Savings: for longer-term goals and earning interest.
  • Protect your PIN, and learn to read your monthly bank statement.
